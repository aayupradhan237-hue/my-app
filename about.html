It is a branch of computer science focused on developing systems that can perform tasks which typically require human cognitive abilities like learning, reasoning, and decision-making. AI technologies include machine learning, natural language processing, and robotics, allowing machines to process vast amounts of data quickly and improve over time with training. AI is integrated into many aspects of daily life, such as in smartphones, voice assistants, streaming service recommendations, and self-driving cars While AI offers significant advancements in productivity and problem-solving across industries like healthcare and finance, its rapid development also raises important ethical concerns about privacy and job displacement
